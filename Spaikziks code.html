Project Proposal

"Spaikziks" website is a dedicated platform that provides artists a space free from the limitations of most social media algorithms that prioritize consistent engagement-driven content over quality; "Spaikziks" allows artists to showcase their work without the need to follow short-form trends or pay and algorithmic suppression for garnering visibility.

The platform is a digital portfolio and marketplace that enables freelance artists to upload, categorize, and promote their work. "Spaiksziks" supports freelancers, including illustrators, painters, and digital artists. With a focus on artistic integrity and equal exposure, "Spaikziks" enables users to store high-quality image hosting, customizable portfolio layouts, direct messages for commissions, and an integrated marketplace where artists can offer custom orders. It enables artist-led discussion between artists through forums and collaborative spaces where Artists can connect, share resources, and support each other. "Spaikziks" compensates artists fairly, unlike other platforms that take excessive cuts. Through the platform, artists can curate their galleries, engaging with their audience directly while setting prices without external interference experienced through other platforms.

License

"Spaikziks" operates under the Creative Commons Attribution NonCommercial-NoDerivatives 4.0 International License, which enables artists to maintain full authority over their artwork. Content distributed under this license enables content to be viewed and shared for personal, non-commercial use but demands correct credit of the original artist's work. The license prohibits modifications and commercial use of content to protect artists' work from unauthorized exploitation. This license safeguards creative efforts while fostering a space for artistic integrity by eliminating the risk of commercial misuse and AI scraping of an artist's ownership rights in such environments. This ensures creators can depend on and trust "Spaikziks" as a platform.
